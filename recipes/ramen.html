John's QD Ramen

Description

This is the Ramen recipe I made for lunch today. Quick and dirty resulting in delicious lunch. I am pretty pretty pretty good!.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 tbsp Miso
  • 90 g dried Udon
  • Delicious Vegetables
  • Chili Flakes
  • Soy Sauce
  • Water or Broth

Steps

  1. Decide that today is the day for delicious RAMEN!
  2. Boil a large pot of water, add Udon and cook to taste ~3 or 4 minutes.
  3. Microwave vegetables to slightly cooked state.
  4. Blend remaining ingredients and microwave to a boil.
  5. Add noodles and broth to vegetables
  6. Enjoy your quick and dirty Ramen
