Favourite Breakfast

Description

This is healthy breakfast eligible for everyday eating. Don't make it if you are a jerk or a pain in the ass or sharing with any of the above.

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp Oil
  • 1 clove Garlic
  • 1/4 large Onion
  • 1/2 cup Vegetable of the day
  • 1 cup cooked long grain brown rice (cold)
  • 1 egg + (1 egg or equivalent eggwhites)
  • Soy Sauce and/or Hot Sauce to taste

Steps

  1. Saute the garlic and onion to get things rolling
  2. Jack up the heat and fire in teh rice, fry until rice is perfectly crispy
  3. Add vegetable of teh day until it's heated through or browned as preferred.
  4. Scramble your egg products and add to pan stirring to keep from sticking
  5. Its ready to come off when the eggs are yrou favourite consistency
  6. Add sauce to taste end enjoy your Favourite Breakfast!
